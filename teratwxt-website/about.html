About

Local, practical, and built around everyday needs

  • No heavy framework required: simple HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Responsive design: adapts to phones, tablets, and desktop screens.
  • Fast inquiry buttons: direct links to WhatsApp and email.
  • Multiple pages: home, shop, services, about, FAQ, and contact.
