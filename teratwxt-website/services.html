Services

Printing, photocopying, scanning & finishing

Teratwxt is not only a store for books and stationery. It also supports students, teachers, offices, churches, and community groups with everyday print and document services.

Available shop services

  • Photocopying: everyday copies for forms, class notes, handouts, and office paperwork.
  • Printing: black-and-white and color jobs, depending on request and machine setup.
  • Scanning: send digital copies of certificates, IDs, forms, and documents.
  • Laminating: protect important papers, signs, notices, and classroom materials.
  • Binding: finish projects, reports, proposals, manuals, and presentation materials.
