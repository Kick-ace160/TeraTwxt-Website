FAQ

Questions customers may ask

These answers help website visitors know how to use the catalog and contact the shop for prices.

Do the product cards show final prices?

No. This website is set up for price inquiries. Customers can tap WhatsApp or email to ask for the latest price and availability.

Can I ask about printing, scanning, or photocopying?

Yes. Teratwxt offers document-related services, and customers can use the contact page or service inquiry buttons to ask for details.

Can more products be added later?

Yes. The website was built so new products can be added in a simple product list inside assets/js/main.js.

Does the website work on mobile phones?

Yes. The layout is responsive and designed to feel easy and clean on smaller screens.

How do I contact the shop quickly?

The fastest option is WhatsApp using the buttons across the website, or email through the contact page.
